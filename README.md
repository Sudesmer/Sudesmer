## 🐲 Dragon's Vault

### PRSE — Predictive Risk & Simulation Engine

A cybersecurity project focused on behavioral risk scoring and short-term threat projection.

The system analyzes authentication-related signals and transforms them into a structured risk score.  
Its purpose is to move beyond simple threshold-based alerts and explore how security events can be evaluated with more context.

Focus areas:

- Windows authentication events
- failed logon behavior
- risk scoring logic
- success-after-fail signals
- threat projection
- detection-oriented thinking

---
